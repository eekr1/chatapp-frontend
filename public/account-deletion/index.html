TalkX Account Deletion

This page explains how users can request deletion of their TalkX account and associated data.

English

You can request account deletion directly in the app:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Go to Delete Account
  3. Enter your current password
  4. Type confirmation text: DELETE MY ACCOUNT (or app-localized equivalent)
  5. Submit deletion request

If you cannot access your account, send a deletion request email to: brenth351@gmail.com

Include your username and a brief request statement from the email you want us to use for follow-up.

  3. Mevcut sifrenizi girin

Hesaba erisemiyorsaniz su adrese e-posta gonderebilirsiniz: brenth351@gmail.com
